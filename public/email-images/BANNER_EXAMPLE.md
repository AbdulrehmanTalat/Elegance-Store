# Email Banner Image Examples

## Banner Specifications

### Dimensions
- **Width**: 600 pixels
- **Height**: 200 pixels
- **Aspect Ratio**: 3:1 (wide and short)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 200KB recommended

## Content Ideas

- Artfully arranged lingerie on elegant fabric

## Design Tips

1. **Keep it Simple**: Don't overcrowd the banner
2. **Brand Colors**: Use your store's color scheme (#ec4899 pink)
3. **Text Overlay (Optional)**: You can add text like "Elegance Store" but keep it minimal
4. **High Quality**: Use professional, high-resolution images
5. **Mobile Friendly**: Remember it will be viewed on mobile devices too
